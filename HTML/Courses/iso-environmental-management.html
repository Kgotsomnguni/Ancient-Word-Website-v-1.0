ISO INTEGRATED ENVIRONMENTAL MANAGEMENT

  • Duration: 10 days
  • CPD Points: 5
  • Cost: R5000

Prerequisite

Participants should have a basic understanding of environmental management principles and systems to fully benefit from this integrated course

Outline

This comprehensive course combines key elements from various ISO environmental management standards:

ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management Systems:

Framework for establishing, maintaining, and improving a Quality Management System (QMS), including quality planning, risk management, and performance evaluation.

ISO 14001:2015 Requirements:

Framework for establishing, maintaining, and improving an Environmental Management System (EMS), including environmental objectives, audits, and compliance.

ISO 14004:2016 Guidelines:

Practical steps for implementing ISO 14001, including stakeholder engagement and documentation requirements.

ISO 14005:2020 Phased Implementation:

Strategies for the gradual implementation of an EMS, setting incremental goals, and managing change

ISO 14006:2020 Ecodesign:

: Incorporating ecodesign principles into EMS, improving product sustainability and reducing environmental impact.

ISO 14064-1:2018 GHG Reporting::

Quantifying and reporting greenhouse gas emissions and removals, including methods for GHG inventories and impact assessment.

Benefits

Participants will gain a comprehensive understanding of environmental management systems, including the establishment, maintenance, and enhancement of ISO 14001:2015. They will learn to effectively implement key requirements, manage environmental impacts, and integrate ecodesign principles to improve product sustainability. The course also covers phased EMS implementation and GHG reporting for accurate quantification and reporting of emissions. This will enhance their ability to create, manage, and optimise an EMS, supporting their professional growth with CPD points and certification.

Assessment

Participants will sit a 3-hour written assessment at an invigilated venue at the end of the course. A minimum pass mark of 60% is required.

Appropriate For

Environmental managers, compliance officers, sustainability professionals, and consultants involved in implementing and managing environmental management systems.
