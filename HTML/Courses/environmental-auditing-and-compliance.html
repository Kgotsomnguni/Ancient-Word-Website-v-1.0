ENVIRONMENTAL AUDITING AND COMPLIANCE COURSE

  • Duration: 4 days
  • CPD Points: 3
  • Cost: R 1500

Prerequisite

Basic knowledge of environmental laws and regulations is required.

Outline

This course provides a detailed understanding of environmental auditing processes and compliance requirements. It covers audit planning, execution, and reporting, as well as strategies for addressing non-compliance issues

Benefits

Participants will develop skills for conducting effective environmental audits, ensuring adherence to regulations, and improving environmental performance. Certification and CPD points will support their professional growth in environmental auditing.

Assessment

Participants will complete a practical audit simulation and written test, with a minimum pass mark of 60%.

Appropriate For

Environmental auditors, compliance officers, and regulatory specialists
