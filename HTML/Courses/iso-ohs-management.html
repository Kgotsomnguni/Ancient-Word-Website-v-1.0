ISO INTEGRATED OHS MANAGEMENT

  • Duration: 10 days
  • CPD Points: 5
  • Cost: R5000

Prerequisite

Participants should have a basic understanding of occupational health and safety principles and management systems to fully benefit from this course.

Outline

This integrated course combines key aspects of occupational health and safety management systems from various standards. It includes:

ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management Systems:

Framework for establishing, maintaining, and improving a Quality Management System (QMS), including quality planning, risk management, and performance evaluation.

ISO 45001:2018 Requirements:

Establishing, maintaining, and improving an OHSMS, including hazard identification, risk assessment, and compliance with legal requirements.

ISO 45002:2022 Guidelines:

Practical implementation guidance for ISO 45001, stakeholder engagement, and documentation requirements.

ISO 45005:2020 Guidelines:

Managing health and safety during the COVID-19 pandemic, including risk assessment, preventive measures, and response planning.

ISO 31000:2018 Risk Management:

Principles and framework for managing risk, risk assessment techniques, and risk treatment.

ISO 45006:2021 Occupational Health Management:

Integrating occupational health into the OHSMS, including health surveillance and strategies for promoting workplace wellness.

Benefits

Participants will develop a thorough understanding of occupational health and safety management, including the effective implementation of ISO 45001:2018. They will learn to manage health and safety during pandemics with ISO 45005:2020, integrate risk management with ISO 31000:2018, and incorporate occupational health into their OHSMS using ISO 45006:2021. The course will enhance their skills in establishing a structured OHSMS, addressing health crises, and promoting workplace wellness. CPD points and certification will support their professional advancement and qualifications.

Assessment

Participants will sit a 3-hour assessment at an invigilated venue at the end of the course. A minimum pass mark of 60% is required.

Appropriate For

Health and safety managers, compliance officers, risk managers, occupational health professionals, and consultants involved in implementing, managing, and improving health and safety management systems.
