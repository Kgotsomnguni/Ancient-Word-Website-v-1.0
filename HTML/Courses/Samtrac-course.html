SAMTRAC

  • Duration: 10 days
  • CPD Points: 5
  • Cost: R5000

Prerequisite

Participants should have a foundational understanding of health and safety principles to engage effectively with the SAMTRAC program.

Outline

This comprehensive course covers key aspects of safety management, including risk assessment, safety policies, and implementation strategies. SAMTRAC provides a thorough understanding of safety management systems and practices, enabling participants to develop and manage effective safety programs within their organizations.

Benefits

Participants will gain a deep understanding of safety management principles and practices, equipping them to create and implement effective safety programs. The course offers practical tools and strategies for managing workplace safety, enhancing their ability to prevent incidents and ensure compliance. Certification and CPD points will strengthen their professional credentials and demonstrate advanced expertise in safety management.

Assessment

A written examination and practical assessment will be conducted at the end of the course, requiring a minimum pass mark of 60%.

Appropriate For

Safety managers, HSE professionals, and compliance officers seeking to enhance their skills in safety management and program implementation.
